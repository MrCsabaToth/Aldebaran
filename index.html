Aldebaran visits Fresno Jan 9th and 10th

Robots.

They've been in our mind for more than a century. Books and movies fed our minds and imagination. And today, they start spreading all over the world, and everybody talks about them, from start-ups and crowdfunding campaigns to big names like Google or Amazon.

Aldebaran, part of SoftBank Robotics, has been building robots for a decade now: NAO and Roméo first, and now Pepper, which was released in Japan in 2014 and is about the hit the US. During this talk, we'll explain why and how robots are going to change the world, and how you can be part of this revolution.
